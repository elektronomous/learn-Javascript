[OPERATORS]
= Operators are syntax which allows us to manipulate value.
= Operators are generally operation that you use on the math lesson:

   Addition
    +---+
    | + |  => Add two numbers
    +---+
   
 Substraction
    +---+
    | - |  => Substract two numbers
    +---+

Multiplication
    +---+
    | * |  => Multiply two numbers
    +---+

  Division
    +---+
    | / |  => Divide two numbers
    +---+    

  Modulation
    +---+
    | % |  => Divide two numbers, yield reminder
    +---+

=
